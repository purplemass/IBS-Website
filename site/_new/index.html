The Project

The Iranian Business School is a philanthropically-motivated project aimed at creating a world-class business school in Iran, which will educate and prepare Iranian men and women to become business leaders capable of transforming the countryâ€™s economic landscape.

There is a profound strategic need for greater management know-how and expertise in Iran to meet the challenges of a rapidly developing nation located in the heart of one of the most exciting and fast growing regions of the world. Our aim is to create a centre of excellence for learning, sharing knowledge, engaging in research and offering entrepreneurial solutions to the most pressing management needs of the business community in Iran.

The Project is spearheaded by a group of individuals from the business community of the Iranian Diaspora and supported by leading private sector figures within Iran.

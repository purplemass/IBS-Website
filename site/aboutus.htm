The Iranian Business School is a philanthropically-motivated project aimed at creating a world-class business school in Iran, which will educate and prepare Iranian men and women to become business leaders capable of transforming the country’s economic landscape. 

There is a profound strategic need for greater management know-how and expertise in Iran to meet the challenges of a rapidly growing country in one of the most exciting and fast changing regions of the world. Our aim is to create a centre of excellence for learning, sharing knowledge, engaging in research and offering entrepreneurial solutions to the most pressing management needs of the business community in Iran.

The project is spearheaded by a group of individuals from the business community of the Iranian Diaspora and supported by leading private sector figures within Iran.

 Mission  
 
 

The mission of IBS is to establish and maintain a superior teaching and research environment – not motivated by profit – for sharing knowledge, achieving growth and nurturing leadership, where;

 
 
   
-
 students are rewarded for assuming responsibility and learning through decision making and action, and;
-
 independent thinking, creativity and self-reliance are the individual traits promoted alongside the social values of fair-play, integrity and equal opportunity.

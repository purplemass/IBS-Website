The Founders of the Iranian Business School Project believe that the establishment of the School as a national centre of management excellence is a valuable contribution to the future of Iran and its development must thus be viewed as a valuable gift to future generations.  The Founders have invested in the project through a charitable vehicle, with no expectation of any return or recovery and all people currently involved with the project (except for operational employees) dedicate their time and services to the project at no cost.

If you wish to assist IBS in any way, please contact us so that we can discuss our vision and plans with you in greater detail.

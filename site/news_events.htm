An Evening to Remember

The Iranian Business School Project will be formally launched at a fundraising event in London on 16th January, 2009. We have been overwhelmed by the degree of enthusiasm and co-operation shown by many individuals and institutions to make this event, and ultimately the Project as a whole a great success.

There will also be an auction of a number of works from contemporary Iranian artists for the benefit of this project, as well as musical entertainment.

The event will take place in the magnificent ballroom of Jumeirah Carlton hotel and is organized and managed by ISM Management, a UK registered charity, specifically formed for global fundraising in support of the Iranian Business School Project.

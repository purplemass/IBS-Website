Fundraising Event

The Iranian Business School Project hosted its Annual Fundraising Event on February 5, 2010 at the Ballroom of The Dorchester Hotel in London.

The evening’s highlights included a keynote speech from global business leader, Mr. Omid Kordestani, Senior Advisor to the Office of the CEO and Founders at Google and remarks from Dr. Ali Mashaheykhi, The President of the Board of Governors of the School.

The event also featured an exclusive auction of a number of significant works from world-renowned Iranian artists including Mr. Parviz Tanavoli. The auction will be presided over by a member of Sotheby’s auction team for the second year.
